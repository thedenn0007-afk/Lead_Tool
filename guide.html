📖 How To Use This Tool

Read this once — then any employee can find and manage leads in 15 minutes

🔍 Step 1 — Find Businesses

Go to Find Leads. Select a business type from the suggestions (dental clinic, architect, etc.) and pick a city. Optionally pick a specific area for more focused results.

Pro tip: Always add an area (e.g. "Koramangala") — it gives 80–150 focused results instead of scattered ones across the whole city.
1
Tap a business type chip (e.g. Dental Clinic)
2
Tap your city (e.g. Bangalore)
3
Tap an area (e.g. Koramangala) — shown automatically
4
Click Open in Google Maps — the search opens in a new tab

⚡ Step 2 — Scrape with Extension

Install the free Instant Data Scraper Chrome extension once. Then use it on any Google Maps results page to extract all businesses automatically.

Install Instant Data Scraper (Free)
1
On Google Maps, scroll the left panel slowly until you see 80–200+ businesses loaded (takes ~30 seconds of scrolling)
2
Click the Instant Data Scraper icon in your Chrome toolbar (top right)
3
It auto-detects the business table. Click Start Crawling — it scrolls and collects everything automatically
4
When done, click Download CSV. Save the file to your desktop.
5
Go to Find Leads → Import CSV and drop the file in. Done.
Expected: 1 search = 80–150 businesses. Run 10 searches = 800–1500 leads in one hour.

📋 Step 3 — Save and Export Your Best Leads

After importing, use Settings to export your full database or only businesses without websites. That keeps your outreach list clean without needing a separate leads page.

1
Import CSV in Find Leads or Auto Scrape
2
Open Settings to review database totals
3
Click Export No-Website CSV to get only businesses without websites
4
Use Export All Leads CSV anytime for backup or team sharing

📞 Step 4 — Work the Exported List

Use the exported CSV from Settings as your call sheet in Excel, Google Sheets, or your team's CRM. The app's job is to find, scrape, import, and export clean lead lists.

1
Export No-Website CSV from Settings for the highest-priority outreach list
2
Open the CSV in Excel or Google Sheets
3
Call through the list and add your own notes in the sheet or CRM
4
Return to LeadTool whenever you want to import fresh batches or export a newer list

✅ Daily Employee Checklist

Give this to anyone on your team. They can follow this every day without training.

Open Find Leads → pick a business type + area → open in Maps
Scroll Maps results until 100+ businesses are loaded
Run Instant Data Scraper → download CSV
Import CSV into Find Leads → Import
Open Settings → export No-Website CSV
Open the export in Sheets or Excel and work through the list
Call 10–20 leads and keep notes in your sheet or CRM
Export a fresh list after each new import batch
